## Men
- Casual

- Event
- Streetwear

## Why categories (not brands) for MVP
Categories let users browse quickly and give us clean, consistent labels for testing the swap flow. Brand-based organization adds dependency on brand coverage and tagging accuracy, which slows iteration without improving the core try-on experience.

## Future expansion
We can add brands, vibes (aesthetic tags), and recommendation/ranking later as additional filters and personalization layers—without changing the core upload → generate flow.
